Detailed Description

Samples the random real numbers according to continuous Uniform distribution.

It has parameters min and max. It produces real numbers in [min, max), i.e. includes min but excludes max.

PDF:
\(f(x)=\frac{1}{\text{max}-\text{min}}\)

Usage:

UniformDistribution dist(0, 1);
dist.next();
