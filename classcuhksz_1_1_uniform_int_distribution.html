Detailed Description

Samples the random integer according to discrete Uniform distribution.

It has parameters min and max. It produces integer in [min, max), i.e. includes min but excludes max.

PMF:
\(f(x)=\frac{1}{\text{max}-\text{min}}\)

Usage:

UniformIntDistribution dist(0, 10);
dist.next();
