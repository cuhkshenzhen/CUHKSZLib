Detailed Description

Samples the random real numbers according to Student's t-distribution.

It has parameter r, the degrees of freedom.

PDF:
\(f(x)=\frac{\Gamma\left(\frac{r+1}{2}\right)}{\sqrt{r\pi}\Gamma(r/2)} \left(1+\frac{x^2}{r}\right)^{-\frac{r+1}{2}}\)

Usage:

StudentTDistribution dist(1);
dist.next();
