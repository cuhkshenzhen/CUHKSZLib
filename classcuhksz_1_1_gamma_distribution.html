Detailed Description

Samples the random real numbers according to Gamma distribution.

It has k as the shape parameter and theta as the scale parameter. See the PDF part for the details of the parameters.

PDF:
\(f(x)=\frac{1}{\Gamma(k)\theta^k}x^{k-1}e^{-x/\theta}\)

where \(\theta\) is the theta parameter.

Usage:

GammaDistribution dist(9, 0.5);
dist.next();
