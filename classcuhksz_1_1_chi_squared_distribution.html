Detailed Description

Samples the random real number according to Chi-squared distribution.

It has parameter r, the "degrees of freedom" parameter. The Chi-squared distribution is a special case of Gamma distribution as \(k=r/2\) and \(\theta=2\). So this class is also a subclass of GammaDistribution.

PDF:
\(f(x)=\frac{1}{\Gamma(\frac{r}{2})2^{r/2}}x^{r/2-1}e^{-x/2}\)

Usage:

ChiSquaredDistribution dist(3);
dist.next();
