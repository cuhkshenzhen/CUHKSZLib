Detailed Description

Samples the random integer according to Poisson distribution.

It has parameter lambda, the "mean" or the expected value.

PMF:
\(f(x)=\frac{\lambda^xe^{-\lambda}}{x!}\)

where \(\lambda\) is the parameter lambda

Usage:

PoissonDistribution dist(5);
dist.next();
